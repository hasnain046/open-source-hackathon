## Project Details

**Project Title:**
InflationIQ – AI-Powered Inflation Forecasting & Economic Intelligence Platform

**Project Description:**
InflationIQ is a full-stack AI-powered economic intelligence platform designed to forecast inflation trends, analyze CPI data, monitor economic news, evaluate currency impacts, and provide explainable AI insights. The platform combines machine learning forecasting, SHAP explainability, News Intelligence, Currency Prediction, RAG-powered document retrieval, AI Economist Copilot, and automated alerting into a single platform.

### Key Features

* AI Inflation Forecast Engine (Prophet + XGBoost)
* News Intelligence & Sentiment Analysis
* Currency Prediction Engine (LSTM + XGBoost)
* SHAP Explainability Dashboard
* AI Economist Copilot
* RAG Knowledge Base
* CPI Analytics & Historical Trends
* Regional Inflation Heatmaps
* Scenario Shock Simulator
* Smart Alerts & Notifications
* Authentication & User Management
